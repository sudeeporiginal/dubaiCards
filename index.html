DUBAI NOL Cards

The NOL cards used in DUBAI is similar to credit and debit cards used in INDIA. The NOL card of dubai is another type of MetroCard, OysterCard and Hola BCN used in DUBAI. This NOL Card gives you pretty much access to all modes of transport in DUBAI. The dubai metro are the one of the best well connected public transport and it also saves your journey time from the traffic sometimes named as "Dubai traffic".
